About BMI

BMI indicates body mass index. It is a screening method to know the status of weight relative to the height. BMI is calculated by weight of person in kilogram(kg) divided by the square of height measured in meter(„é°).

BMI method catogrize the weight of person relative to thier height in Underweight, Healthyweight, Overweight and Obesity.
