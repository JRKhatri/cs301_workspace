About BMI

BMI fullform is body mass index. It is a screening method to know the status weight relative to your height. BMI is calculated by weight of person in kilogram(kg) divided by the square of height measured in meter(m^2).

BMI method catogrize the weight of person relative to thier height in Underweight, Healthyweight, Overweight and Obesity.
